Corned Beef Hash

Description

This corned beef hash is a savory combination of corned beef, potatoes, and onions. A quick and easy meal using leftover or canned corned beef.

Ingredients

  • 6 large potatoes, peeled and diced
  • 1 (12 ounce) can corned beef, cut into chunks
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 1 cup beef broth

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Combine potatoes, corned beef, onion, and broth in a large, deep skillet over medium heat. Cover and simmer until potatoes are soft enough to mash and liquid is almost gone.
  3. Mix well and serve hot.
